What vaccines should I ask family to get before seeing a newborn?

When it comes to vaccines for family members before seeing a newborn, the main one to consider is the Tdap vaccine. Maternal Tdap vaccination during pregnancy is 90% protective against whooping cough in infants under 2 months and is by far the most important preventive measure.

Is it safe to kiss newborns?

It’s best to avoid people kissing your baby’s face in the first six to eight weeks, when the risk of infection is highest. After that, as babies grow and their immune systems strengthen, the risks decline.
